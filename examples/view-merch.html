Distribute Back to School Promotional Flyers

The custom flyer promotes the best yearbook deal of the school year with information customized to your school yearbook: price, promotion dates and your school name.

Make sure that everyone knows about this low price offer by distributing the flyer to everyone. If you have not received a copy of your school's custom flyer contact your sales representative or plant consultant and they will send it to you.

The flyer advertises the lowest yearbook price of the year. Make sure that everyone knows about this low price offer by distributing the flyer to everyone. If you have not received a copy of your school's custom flyer contact your sales representative or plant consultant and they will send it to you.

  • Include the flyer in student registration packets or mailings to the home
  • Ask the office to attach an electronic copy of the flyer to emails that are sent home to parents
  • Use social media and post the flyer on Facebook or Twitter
  • Leave copies of the flyer where parents and students will see it. This includes the school office, the ticket counter at school sporting events and even the concession stand counter

Contact your Jostens representative for a copy of your custom flyer.

Social Media Marketing Introduction

Many yearbook staffs have fun and success marketing their yearbook using social media.

Take a look at the Yearbook Social Media Marketing Guide and Social Media video to determine how your staff can best use social media to promote the yearbook at your school.

View the Guide
Hang Back to School Posters

You will find posters in your Blitz Kit specifically designed for the Back to School Blitz.

Hang these in high-traffic areas: main office, school entrance or cafeteria. Be sure to fill in the yearbook savings and Back to School offer deadline. This will ensure everyone knows how much money they will save if they buy before the deadline.

Use the two “Design Your Own” Posters to promote your Facebook page, any contest you are running or any message that will get students and parents excited about buying the yearbook. Do you need inspiration for your "Design Your Own" Posters?

View Poster Ideas
Hand out Business Cards

The business cards included in your Blitz Kit are a perfect way to promote ReplayIt®.

With ReplayIt, the entire school can contribute photos to the yearbook staff. They cards are easy to hand out to students and parents and contain all the info needed to get them started using ReplayIt.

  • Attend school-sponsored events and hand out to people standing in line at the ticket counter or concession stand.
  • Set up a yearbook table during parent/teacher conferences and hand out cards to parents.
  • Ask the school office staff to include a business card in home mailings.
Reorder Cards Print Your Own Cards
Display Yard Signs

Yard Signs are a great way to remind your school community that the yearbook is on sale. During the Back to School Blitz place the yard signs in high traffic areas.

  • Sporting events
  • Parent Night or Student Orientation
  • School Parking Lot
Play the Jostens Yearbook Commercial

This commercial is a catchy and fun way to let students and parents at your school know that yearbooks are on sale.

  • At a school pep rally
  • During the school’s video announcements
  • On the big screen before, during or after a school sponsored event
  • Post on Facebook and Twitter, don’t forget to include the information on the Blitz end date and a link to jostensyearbooks.com

Add Web Banner/Widget to School Website

Use a web banner or widget to effectively promote your yearbook sale on the web.

Placing a web banner ad or image widget on your school’s homepage is a great way to let students and parents know that the yearbook is on sale and easy to purchase.

Social Media Marketing Idea: Use the web banners as a part of your social media marketing, post them on your Facebook page and tweet them out.

Meet with Fall Coaches and Club Leaders

Custom invitations can be used to invite leaders of different clubs and organizations at your school to take part in using ReplayIt to get more coverage of members in the yearbook.

Print out custom invitations and hand deliver them to the Fall Coaches and Club Leaders, make sure to explain how ReplayIt works.

Great Idea: Take this task to the next level by assigning staff members to present to the different sports teams, clubs and organizations in your school. The presentation could include staff members giving an overview of the yearbook, explaining and demonstrating the ReplayIt app and handing out business cards.

School Announcements

Use the school announcements to let people know that the yearbook includes a FREE digital Time Capsule.

Let everyone in school know that the yearbook is on sale. Create your own announcement script or provide a prewritten script to the organizers of your school announcements. We've written several scripts that you can easily download. If you write your own make sure to promote a reason to buy the yearbook and ways students can connect with your staff, like social media.

Announcement script ideas are available in the Digital Classroom.
