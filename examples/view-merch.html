Distribute Back to School Promotional Flyer

The Back to School Flyer promotes the best yearbook deal of the school year. It advertises the lowest yearbook price of the year.

Make sure that everyone knows about this low price offer by distributing the flyer to everyone. If you have not received a copy of your school's custom flyer contact your sales representative or plant consultant and they will send it to you.

  • Include the flyer in student registration packets or mailings to the home
  • Ask the office to attach an electronic copy of the flyer to emails that are sent home to parents
  • Use social media and post the flyer on Facebook or Twitter
  • Leave copies of the flyer where parents and students will see it. This includes the school office, the ticket counter at school sporting events and even the concession stand counter
Social Media Marketing Introduction

Staffs across the country have fun and success marketing their yearbook through social media. Review the social media marketing guide and view the video below to determine how your staff can best use social media to promote the yearbook at your school.

Hang Back to School Posters

You will find posters in your Blitz Kit specifically designed for the Back to School Blitz. Grab a marker and fill in the savings and Back to School offer deadline. Hang the posters in high-traffic areas like the main office, school entrance or cafeteria.

You will also find two Design Your Own Posters where you can promote a yearbook sales message that is specific to your school. Message ideas include the promotion of your social media website, promotion of a special contest that you are running or an event that you are hosting to promote the yearbook sale to parents and students.

Do you need inspiration for your Design Your Own posters?

Visit the Resource
Display Yard Signs

Yard Signs are a great way to remind your school community that the yearbook is on sale. During the Back to School Blitz place the yard signs in high traffic areas.

  • Sporting events
  • Parent Night or Student Orientation
  • School Parking Lot
Hand out Business Cards

The business cards included in your Blitz Kit are a perfect way to promote the yearbook sale. They are easy to hand out to students and parents and contain all the info needed to get them started using ReplayIt.

  • Attend school sponsored events and hand out to parents and student standing in line at the ticket counter or at the concession stand.
  • Setup a yearbook table during parent/teacher conferences and hand out cards to parents in attendance.
  • Ask the school office staff to include a business card in mailings to the home
