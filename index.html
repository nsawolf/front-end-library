Components

Components are pieces of the library. They are reusable parts to make something bigger: a page, a column, etc... The types of Components are:

  • Common: Aka elements. li, p, h1, h2, table, etc...
  • Comp:Bootstrap: Chunks of HTML/CSS that is defined by Bootstrap, and restyled for our needs. May include JS plugins.
  • Comp:Custom: Chunks of HTML/CSS that is defined and styled by us. May be based off of Comp:Bootstrap components. May include custom JS.

Views

Views are single page parts of an experience. Not designed to be a click-through prototype. Their purposes mirror that of the Examples.
